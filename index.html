About Me

Hi there! My name is Patrick and I am 28 years old. I grew up and live in Switzerland. For the last past six years, I worked as a ‘Network Security Engineer’ in a small company with customers from all around the world. Before that, I worked six more years in the field of IT (4 years education, 2 years as System Administrator), which sums up to a total of almost 12 years in the Information Technology sector. My character can be described as ambitious, dynamic, flexible, and always interested in learning something new. In my free time, I like to travel and discover the world, its landscapes, and especially its cultures. In 2019 I traveled through Central America and recently I volunteered for three months in Ecuador, South America. To combine these two passions (traveling and my profession) I decided to start working as a freelancer. If you want to know more about me, check out my skills below or look for me on social media!
